Visual Effects Test Page

Click each button to see the different disappearing effects

Effect Descriptions

  • Explode: Characters burst outward in all directions with gravity
  • Float Away: Characters gently float upward and fade away
  • Spiral: Characters spiral outward in a circular pattern
  • Dissolve: Characters break into small particles and fade
  • Wave: Characters move in a wave pattern before disappearing
  • Vortex: Characters get pulled into a spinning vortex
  • Gravity Pull: Characters fall downward like they're being pulled by gravity
  • Firework: Characters shoot upward then explode like fireworks
  • Scatter: Characters scatter randomly in all directions
  • Typewriter Erase: Characters delete one by one like a typewriter
